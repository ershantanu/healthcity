RESPIRATORY MEDICINE, ALLERGY & SLEEP DISORDERS

Pulmonology department aims helping patients with Obstructive Pulmonary Disease to slow the progression of the disease and improve the quality of life.

The Department of Respiratory Medicine, Allergy and Sleep disorders is one of the most comprehensive units dealing with an entire range of respiratory conditions including asthma and allergic diseases, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), lung infections including tuberculosis, interstitial lung diseases, pulmonary hypertension, sleep disordered breathing and lung malignancies.

Our Best Services Included

  • FIBRE OPTIC BRONCHOSCOPY
  • RIGID BRONCHOSCOPY
  • Diagnosis of Tuberculosis and other infections
  • Smoking Cessation Clinic
  • Evaluation of undiagnosed Pleural Effusions
  • Allergy, Asthma clinic with allergen testing and immunotherapy
  • ENDOBRONCHIAL UNTRASOUND (EBUS) & TRANSBRONCHIAL NEEDLE ASPIRATION
  • Diagnosis of Mediastinal Adenopathy – TB, Sarcoidosis, Lymphoma, Malignancy
  • Exercise training for chronic respiratory diseases including COPD, Bronchiestasis, ILD, c/c Asthma
  • Bronchial Artery Embolisation for Hemoptysis

The EBUS facility at the centre is the latest minimally invasive procedure used to diagnose lung cancer, infections and other diseases. Various other medical procedures of international standards such as Thoracoscopy, Rigid Bronchoscopy, Fibreoptic Bronchoscopy are also offered for better care. The doctors are well experienced in the diagnosis and treatment of various airway and lung diseases like asthma, COPD, Pulmonary infection, Pleural diseases etc. Services of specialised clinics such as allergy & asthma clinic, smoking & cessation clinic and lung cancer screening clinic, etc. provide personalised care for a comprehensive range of health issues.

Patients with COPD can enjoy a broad and comprehensive pulmonary rehabilitation program at The HealthCity hospital so they can manage their symptoms, improve muscle strength and feel stronger and fitter. The doctors at The HealthCity hospital advise the patients on what kind of exercises they must do and what they should avoid. Pulmonary Rehabilitation program comes as a course spread over a certain period of time, say 8 weeks or even 12 weeks, depending on the seriousness of the condition and during this time, they would teach the patients how to manage their life without becoming breathless. Unique needs of each patient are taken into account, rather than giving a generic treatment.

The Healthcity Hospital

A unit of Prtihraji Devi Healthcare System Pvt. Ltd.

The Healthcity Hospital – A unit of Prtihraji Devi Healthcare System Pvt. Ltd. has been established in 2017, by virtue of dedicated team of Neurosurgeons, Neurologists, Neurointerventional surgeons, paramedics and world class equipment and facilities. The Healthcity Hospital - a multispecialty hospital and corporate venture with co-operative spirit is situated at Saraikazi, Harahua, Varanasi, with 50-inpatient bed strength at present with various Super specialties, especially in the field of Neurosurgery. The Hospital is ideally located and approachable by public transport from all the sides. “The Healthcity” Advanced multi-specialty center for Neurosurgery, Gynecology surgery, Orthopedic surgery & General surgery.
Our Mission

Faithful to our tradition, we provide the highest possible standard of care and treatment in a Professional and compassionate manner to every person who avails of our services

INFRASTRUCTURE DETAILS

The Hospital provides a wide spectrum of Super-specialty Services in the field of Neurosurgery, Neurology, Orthopedics including Spine Surgery, Joint Replacement & Sports Medicine, Urology, Nephrology, Gastroenterology & Surgical Gastroenterology, process driven Critical Care services, along with the most advanced Diagnostic & Imaging facilities, provide the necessary backbone for patient care for holistic, comprehensive & contemporary care to patients.

STATE OF- THE ART- EQIPMENTS

The Hospital has one of the most well equipped labs for entire gamut of diagnostic services in Hematology, Biochemistry, Microbiology and Serology. It has 24 hrs Laboratory with Automated analyzers to perform all Biochemistry, ELISA, Pathology, Microbiology investigations.
